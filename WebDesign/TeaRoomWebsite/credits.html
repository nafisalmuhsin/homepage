Security considerations

In order to minimise security risks, many steps can be taken. For example, customer data should not be shared with any unauthorised person/third party company, especially without customers consent. This is not just an important secuirty measure, but it is also a legal issue with data protection and privacy. Moreover, the view request info should only be accessed by the admin of the buisness to prevent misuse of customer details. I suggest a login with a strong password with both upper case and lowercase letters and symbols. the password should be minimum 8 characters.



Another thing i suggest for the company to do, is to have an official company email. I.e. info@chollertontearooms.com. This will help customers easily identify emails sent by the company, and protect them from clicking phishing emails that are pretending to be this company.
